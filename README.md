📞 Calling Agents Performance Dashboard – Excel 2026

An interactive Excel dashboard built to analyze 1,000 customer service calls handled by 5 agents across 3 Pakistani cities. The project transforms raw call center data into actionable business insights around agent performance, revenue, customer satisfaction, and operational scheduling.

🎯 Project Objective

Call centers generate massive amounts of data daily — but without structure, it's just noise. This project was built to answer real business questions:

    Which agents are driving the most revenue?
    When are call volumes highest — and are we staffed for it?
    Where is customer satisfaction falling short?
    How do performance patterns differ by city and gender?

📊 Dataset Overview
Column	Description
Call Number	Unique call ID
Customer ID	Linked customer reference
Duration	Call length in minutes
Representative	Agent ID (R01–R05)
Representative Name	Agent full name
Date of Call	Date the call occurred
Purchase Amount	Revenue generated from the call
Satisfaction Rating	Customer rating (0–5 scale)
Day of Week	Day the call was made
Duration Bucket	Grouped call length category
City	Customer city (Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad)
Gender	Customer gender

Total Records: 1,000 calls | Agents: 5 | Cities: 3 | Time Period: Full Year 2023
🔍 Key Insights

📅 Monthly Trends

    Call volume peaked in September (30) and February (28)
    December had the lowest activity with only 9 calls
    Insight: Staffing can be proactively scaled up in high-volume months

📆 Day-of-Week Patterns

    Wednesday (38 calls) and Sunday (34 calls) are the busiest days
    Saturday (25 calls) is consistently the slowest
    Insight: Agent schedules should be optimized around mid-week and weekend demand

💰 Revenue Metrics

    Total purchase amount across all calls: $96,623
    Average revenue per call: ~$96.62
    Insight: Higher-rated calls tend to correlate with higher purchase amounts

🌆 City & Gender Breakdown

    Lahore has the highest customer volume: 326F / 206M
    Karachi shows a near-even split: 144F / 129M
    Islamabad skews female-heavy: 132F / 63M
    Insight: Agent communication and product messaging can be regionalized

⭐ Customer Satisfaction

    Overall average rating: 3.9 / 5
    5-star ratings: 307 out of 1,000 (30.7%)
    Insight: Nearly 70% of calls fall below top-tier satisfaction — a clear coaching opportunity

👤 Agent-Level Performance

    All 5 agents compared on: call count, total duration, and total purchase amount
    Individual monthly rating trends tracked (e.g., Bilal Khan: 3.5 in Jan → 4.2 in Jun)
    Insight: Rating dips mid-to-late year suggest fatigue or process gaps worth investigating
